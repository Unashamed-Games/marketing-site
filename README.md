## Vision

To build a trusted portfolio of high-quality games that people of all ages can enjoy—where integrity, excellence, and truth are never compromised.

## Mission

We create fun, engaging, and wholesome games that reflect the truth of the Gospel of Jesus Christ—offering a free experience supported by limited, respectful ads, and a simple option for a clean, uninterrupted experience.

---

## Values

### 1. Unashamed of the Gospel

We are unashamed of the Gospel of Jesus Christ, and we reflect that truth in how we build and serve.

- We do not hide what we believe
- We act with honesty, humility, and care
- We let our work reflect our convictions—not just our words

### 2. Player First

We respect the player's time, attention, and trust.

- No interruption of gameplay for monetization
- No unnecessary friction or manipulation
- We design for enjoyment, not compulsion

### 3. Integrity Over Optimization

We choose what is right over what is most profitable.

- No deceptive UI or dark patterns
- No misleading ads or manipulative tactics
- Clear, honest choices and pricing

### 4. Excellence in Craft

We take pride in building things well.

- Clean, polished experiences
- Consistent design across every game
- Reliable performance and thoughtful accessibility

### 5. Wholesome by Design

Our games are safe, appropriate, and enjoyable for all ages.

- No inappropriate content
- No harmful or addictive mechanics
- A product families can trust

### 6. Long-Term Thinking

We build for trust and longevity, not short-term gains.

- Retention over extraction
- Portfolio over one-off success
- Sustainable growth over quick wins

### 7. Simplicity Wins

We favor clarity over complexity.

- Simple gameplay
- Simple monetization
- Simple, maintainable systems
