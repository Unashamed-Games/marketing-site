# Unashamed Games

*Wholesome games, free to play, built with conviction.*

---

## Vision

We want to build a catalog of games that people of all ages can enjoy, where integrity and excellence are the standard, not the exception.

## Mission

We make wholesome games that reflect the truth of the Gospel of Jesus Christ. Every game is free to play with limited ads, and there's always a simple option to go ad-free.

---

## Values

### 1. Unashamed of the Gospel

We don't hide what we believe. The Gospel of Jesus Christ shapes how we build, how we serve, and how we treat people. Our work reflects our convictions, not just our words.

### 2. Player First

We respect your time and attention. Gameplay is never interrupted for ads. There are no manipulative hooks or artificial friction. We build games people want to play, not games that exploit habits.

### 3. Integrity Over Optimization

We pick what's right over what's most profitable. No dark patterns, no misleading ads. Pricing is clear and honest.

### 4. Excellence in Craft

We care about the details. Every game should feel polished, run well, and be accessible to as many people as possible.

### 5. Wholesome by Design

Our games are appropriate for all ages. No inappropriate content, no predatory mechanics. Something you'd be comfortable handing to a kid.

### 6. Long-Term Thinking

We'd rather earn trust over time than chase short-term wins. We're building a portfolio, not a single hit. Growth should be sustainable, not extractive.

### 7. Simplicity Wins

Simple gameplay. Simple monetization. Simple systems we can actually maintain. If it doesn't need to be complicated, it won't be.
